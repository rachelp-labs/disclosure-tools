Clinical Trial Protocol For publication Cat. 2 & 3: at first MSC decision · Cat. 1 paediatric/PIP: with final results · Cat. 1 adult-only: 30 months after end of trial
▼
Upload note
TWO versions required. Upload the "for publication" version first, with personal data and CCI removed, then upload the linked "not for publication" full version. Remove file metadata (file properties / author fields) from both versions before upload.

Publication timing by category: Cat. 2 & 3 — at first MSC decision. Cat. 1 paediatric/PIP — published together with the final summary of results, as soon as results are submitted in CTIS. Cat. 1 adult-only — 30 months after end of trial in the EU/EEA.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • Subject/patient identifiers and personal data
  • Private contact details of investigators (functional/professional details should be retained)
  • Signatures (must always be removed from published version)
  • Commercially confidential manufacturing details
  • Unpublished proprietary assay or biomarker methods
  • IP-sensitive compound or formulation information (pre-authorisation)
Retain in published version
  • Protocol title, version date, and CTIS/EudraCT number
  • Primary and secondary objectives
  • Inclusion and exclusion criteria
  • Study design, endpoints, and statistical methods
  • Investigational product name (INN preferred)
  • Names of principal investigators and head of clinic/institution
  • Professional/functional contact details of principal investigators
  • Sponsor name and functional contact details

Summary of Medicinal Product Characteristics (SmPC) For publication Category 2 & 3 only · At the first MSC decision
▼
Upload note
Category 2 & 3 only: upload the "for publication" SmPC first, then the linked "not for publication" version if needed for the workspace record. ⚠ Do not upload an IB into the SmPC placeholder.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • Draft watermark text or internal headers/footers
  • Commercially sensitive formulation details not in approved labelling
  • Internal version tracking identifiers
Retain in published version
  • All standard SmPC sections 1–20
  • Authorised indications and posology
  • Contraindications and adverse drug reaction data
  • Pharmacological and pharmacokinetic properties
References

Informed Consent Form (ICF) & Patient Information Sheet For publication Category 2 & 3: at the first MSC decision · Category 1: not published
▼
Upload note
Category 1: ONE version only → "not for publication" slot. Category 2 & 3: upload the "for publication" version first, then upload the linked "not for publication" full version.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • Private contact details of investigators (site-specific phone numbers, personal addresses)
  • Signatures (must always be removed from published version)
  • Personal data of trial coordinator or staff (other than PIs)
  • Track-change metadata (submit clean version to "for pub" placeholder)
Retain in published version
  • Plain-language description of the trial purpose
  • Participant rights and voluntary withdrawal procedures
  • Risk and benefit summary
  • Names of principal investigators and head of clinic/institution
  • Professional/functional contact details of principal investigators
  • General sponsor functional contact information
  • Version date and document number
References
EU CTR Art. 25(1)(c)EU CTR Art. 29CTR Annex III, point (f)Revised CTIS Transparency RulesCTIS Sponsor Handbook (v6.2, March 2026)
Recruitment Arrangements & Advertisement Materials For publication Category 2 & 3 only · At the first MSC decision
▼
Upload note
Category 2 & 3 only: upload the "for publication" version first, then upload the linked "not for publication" full version.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • Private contact details of investigators (personal phone numbers, private addresses)
  • Signatures (must always be removed from published version)
  • Internal site identifiers or cost codes
  • Site-specific financial incentive amounts
  • Draft/internal version markers
Retain in published version
  • General description of the study and its purpose
  • Broad eligibility criteria summary
  • Names of principal investigators and head of clinic/institution
  • Professional/functional contact route (e.g. functional email, generic site number)
  • Sponsor name and trial identifier
References
CTR Annex I, Section D(5)CTR Annex III, point (f)Revised CTIS Transparency RulesCTIS Sponsor Handbook (v6.2, March 2026)
Summary of Results & Lay Summary For publication Within 12 months of end of trial (6 months for paediatric/PIP)
▼
Upload note
TWO versions required (all categories): (1) full version → "not for publication" slot; (2) publication version → "for publication" slot. Submitted via the CTIS results module.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • Any individual participant data or subject identifiers
  • Names of individual investigators
  • Commercially sensitive pipeline or strategy information
Retain in published version
  • Plain-language description of trial outcomes
  • High-level safety and efficacy findings (aggregate)
  • General description of trial population
  • Sponsor name and trial registration number

Substantial Modification — Protocol Amendment For publication When the authorised substantial modification becomes public in CTIS
▼
Upload note
For a for publication amendment, upload the "for publication" version first, then the linked "not for publication" version as needed. ⚠ Track-changes version for authority review only — do not upload it to a public-facing slot.
Redaction guide
Redact from published version
  • All same redactions as original protocol apply
  • Track-changes version: for MSC review ONLY
  • Names of reviewers or internal review dates
Retain in published version
  • Reason and justification summary for the modification
  • Updated protocol sections (clean final text only)
  • Modification scope description
